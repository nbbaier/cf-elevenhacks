# Hack #2: Cloudflare | ElevenHacks

Build something creative with Cloudflare's developer platform and ElevenLabs, then submit a high-quality viral-style video demonstrating what you've built.

[Cloudflare Agents](https://agents.cloudflare.com/) is the platform for building AI agents. Build agents on Cloudflare with durable execution, serverless inference, and pricing that scales. Use Workers for compute, Workers AI for inference, Durable Objects for state, and tools like Browser Rendering and Vectorize.

[ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io) offers state-of-the-art voice AI including text-to-speech, voice cloning, and conversational AI agents. Combine Cloudflare's edge infrastructure with ElevenLabs' voice capabilities to build something unique.

## What we're looking for

We're most excited to see creative use of **Cloudflare Workers**, **Durable Objects**, and a combination of **ElevenLabs APIs**. Show us what's possible when you push Cloudflare's infrastructure to its limits.

For less technical participants who aren't able to experiment with the Cloudflare APIs as much, we're still keen to see you submit something deployed on Cloudflare. Use tools like Cursor, Claude Code, Zed, or another AI-assisted coding platform to help you build and deploy something to Cloudflare Workers/Pages.

## Getting started

The [Cloudflare Workers free tier](https://developers.cloudflare.com/workers/platform/pricing/) is very generous and offers almost all the functionality of the paid tier, so you can get started without any cost.
